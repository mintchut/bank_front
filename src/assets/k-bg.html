K-Cash Connect Plus

KWebPageContentHead

​​​​​Financial management freedom.

Manage / monitor transactions from anywhere in real time​​​.

  •  

Key to your financial transactions.

Real time transactions

Scheduled transactions

World standard security

Service Details

Fees
  • Entrance fee : 3,000 baht
  • Annual fee : 2,000 baht per year

How to Apply

Qualifications
  • Must be a company
  • Must have a KBank savings or current account
Required Documents
  • A certified copy of company registration
  • A copy of the identification card of the authorized signatory
  • Letter of Authority
  • A copy of the identification card of the delegate authority
  • A letter of consent (In the case the applicant wants to link another account with the system)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is K-Cash Connect Plus ?

K-Cash Connect Plus is an Online Banking for business to effectively manage their finance.

Who should use this service ?

A company or a group of companies who want to conveniently manage and monitor their transactions online.

Is this a mobile application that I have to install on my mobile phone ?

No, you don’t have to install anything. Cash Connect Plus is an Online Banking, all you need are web browser and internet connection.
